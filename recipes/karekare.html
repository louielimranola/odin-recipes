KareKare Recipe

Kare Kare is a type of Filipino stew with a rich and thick peanut sauce. It is a popular dish in the Philippines served during special occasions. The traditional recipe is composed of ox tailThere are instances wherein both ox tripe and tail are used.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • tomato
  • cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients
  2. Cook Pasta
  3. Mix pasta with sauce
  4. Bake for 20 mins
